What it does

Everything a receptionist does — automatically

From the first ring to the confirmed appointment, AIVoiceConnect handles it all so your team can focus on the people in front of them.

📞

AI Voice Receptionist

Answers inbound calls, greets callers naturally, and guides them through booking — with a warm British voice, any time of day.

💬

WhatsApp Booking

Handles multi-turn WhatsApp conversations to confirm appointments — just like a human would, but available instantly 24/7.

📱

SMS Fallback

If WhatsApp isn't available, replies switch to SMS automatically — so no customer is ever left without a response.

📅

Appointment Management

Bookings appear in your dashboard instantly. Automated reminders sent 24h and 2h before — no-shows dramatically reduced.

Every message in one place. AI-suggested replies help your team respond faster. Resolve conversations with one click.

Missed Call Auto-reply

When a call goes unanswered, a personalised message is sent within seconds — turning missed calls into confirmed bookings.
