What it does

Everything a receptionist handles —
done automatically

From the first ring to the confirmed appointment, your AI receptionist never takes a day off.

📞
AI Voice Receptionist

Answers every inbound call with a warm, natural British voice. Greets callers, understands their needs, and guides them through booking — no hold music, no voicemail.

💬
WhatsApp Booking

Multi-turn conversations that feel human. Patients describe what they need, the AI confirms date, time, and name — and logs the appointment instantly.

📱
SMS Fallback

When WhatsApp isn't available, replies switch to SMS automatically. Zero configuration — every customer gets a response, every time.

🔔
Missed Call Auto-reply

A personalised message lands in seconds after a missed call — turning lost calls into confirmed bookings before your competitor even picks up.

📅
Appointment Management

Every booking surfaces in your dashboard instantly. Automated reminders at 24h and 2h reduce no-shows without any manual chasing.

🗂️
Smart Inbox

All conversations in one place. AI-suggested replies help your team respond to the edge cases in seconds. Resolve with one click.
